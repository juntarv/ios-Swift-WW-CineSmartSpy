Experience the thrill of being a master spy in SmartSpy - an exciting arcade game that challenges your reflexes and timing skills!

GAMEPLAY
Navigate your agent across the screen to catch falling characters while avoiding dangerous obstacles. Each successful catch earns you points and brings you closer to unlocking exclusive rewards. But be careful - you only have three lives, and one wrong move could end your mission!

CORE FEATURES
Fast-paced arcade gameplay with intuitive touch controls
Progressive difficulty that keeps you engaged
Three-life system adds strategic tension
Real-time score tracking
Smooth animations and responsive controls
Portrait mode optimized for one-handed play

CHARACTER SHOP
Collect coins during gameplay to unlock a variety of unique spy character models. Each character brings their own distinct style to your missions. Build your collection and find your favorite agent!

Unlock special achievements based on your gameplay performance. From completing your first mission to achieving perfect runs, each milestone is rewarded. Track your progress and discover all hidden rewards!

WHY SMARTSPY
SmartSpy combines classic arcade gameplay with modern mobile gaming features. The simple yet challenging mechanics make it easy to pick up but hard to master. Whether you're playing for a few minutes or going for a marathon session, SmartSpy delivers consistently engaging entertainment.

The game rewards both quick reflexes and strategic thinking. Should you risk catching that falling agent when an obstacle is nearby? Can you maintain your focus as the game speeds up? Every session presents new challenges and opportunities to beat your personal best.
